WeekWeaver

WeekWeaver is a dynamic scheduling tool designed to help users effortlessly plan and manage their week. With an intuitive interface, users can create, organize, and prioritize tasks, appointments, and events, ensuring a balanced and productive week. Whether for personal use or team collaboration, WeekWeaver empowers users to visualize their commitments, set reminders, and adapt to changing schedules seamlessly.

Learn more

This software uses generative AI to give you the most productive week possible. With the ability to add deadlines, set priorities for tasks, and change your calendar on demand, you can make a new calendar quickly with the support of AI.

Plan your week around you. Don't limit yourself to just planning out tasks for work. Use WeekWeaver to plan out tasks at home too such as painting, cleaning, cooking, or even integrate reading time into your week.
