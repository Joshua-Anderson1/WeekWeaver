Welcome to WeekWeaver!

For anybody who has a lot on their plate and not enough time to plan out how to attack it all, WeekWeaver is a weekly planner that takes in all of your current tasks and assignments and uses generative A.I. to give you an itenerary that gives you an optimized way to tackle your tasks for the week. Unlike most products that simply record a to-do list or lists your due dates, our product intends to give you a solution and a plan of attack for the weeks ahead so that you can stress less and be more productive.

WeekWeaver is, at its core, a weekly planner software that is designed to assist its users with planning for tasks throughout a period of time. The intended design is for users (who may be feeling overwhelmed due to being swamped with work) to input their tasks for a given period of time into the planner. If a task is high priority, the user may mark it as such. The user may also input times that they are unable to work on their tasks so that the planner does not schedule during those inopportune stretches of time such as daily meetings or class times.

Once the user has input the different tasks that must be completed, the software will take into account due dates, task complexity, and high priority listings and develop an itenerary for the user (based around their schedule) that displays the most optimal method of tackling each task set before them including periodic breaks to prevent the user being overworked. This program and its features are intended to take away the stress of planning from the users, granting them an optimized plan that can allow them to complete their tasks in a timely manner before any approaching deadlines.

This product is intended for absolutely anybody! WeekWeaver is designed to be useful to anybody who may need a little extra help in planning their week and wants the stress of planning it all out to be taken off of their shoulders.

The users of WeekWeaver will have less stress, better time management, and deadlines will be easier to meet as the planning is taken out of their week and they can simply perform their necessary tasks in both their professional and personal lives.
